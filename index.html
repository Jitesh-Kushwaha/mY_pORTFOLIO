About

Who is Jitesh?

A robotics, embedded systems, DevOps and space-technology engineer building systems that ship, fly and keep streaming telemetry.

Who I Am

I am a robotics and space-systems engineer working on autonomous drones, rockets, satellite-adjacent systems and DevOps-driven software pipelines. I operate where hardware, firmware and cloud infrastructure meet.

What I Do

I build autonomous flight systems, embedded flight computers, LoRa and RF telemetry networks, sensor fusion stacks, DevOps infrastructure and distributed systems. From board bring-up to CI/CD and cloud dashboards, I design the full loop.

My Focus

Mars-capable drones, rocket avionics, high-altitude BalloonSat experiments, resilient LoRa telemetry, Edge AI for guidance & perception, CI/CD pipelines, Linux, containers, Kubernetes and cloud automation for real hardware.

What Drives Me

I do not optimize click-through rates. I build systems that fly through dust, survive vibration and keep transmitting bits in hostile environments. I care about correctness, observability and engineering discipline more than shiny dashboards.

Operating Principles

  • Measure twice, launch once.
  • Design for failure, instrument everything.
  • Automate what hurts, document what matters.
  • Prefer simple, observable systems over clever abstractions.

Stacks I Live In
